Why is decentralized intelligence important?

Valuable data for AI should not only be in the hands of a few large organizations. It's time for a shift, and decentralizing data collection is the way forward.

Navigate is a decentralized data platform that will liberate your data, rewarding you and its community for helping build new and valuable datasets for AI.

Navigate is the first Web3-native big data platform gamifying and decentralizing the collection of training data. Its mission is to build a decentralized intelligence platform that enables individuals to earn from the valuable data they generate everyday.

The future of AI hinges on two simple variables: compute and data. But we are rapidly exhausting our stockpile of publicly available training data. Navigate is changing this by building a platform that creates and augments datasets for training and tuning AI models.

Data Quest is Navigate's application that gamifies the collection and labeling of data. Users are rewarded for actively contributing high-value training data through quests and passively contributing data streams through data connections.

The Data Quest game collects and augments many types of datasets — from active data contributions like data labeling to datasets from apps you choose to connect such as Amazon or Spotify. You're in control and choose to contribute only what you want to.

We are building Navigate with a privacy-first mentality. Whether you are sharing personal insights or labeling datasets — all data will be anonymized and encrypted according to data security best practices.

Navigators earn points for each and every contribution to the platform. Points will play a big role in the Navigate ecosystem, so stay tuned!
